Conceptual Model

Entities & Attributes:

Steam Account:

  • steamID (primary key)
  • steamEmail
  • steamName
  • steamPhone

Purchase:

  • purchaseId (primary key)
  • steamId (foreign key)
  • purchaseDate
  • productId(foreign Key)

Product

  • productId(primary key)
  • productName
  • productQuantity

Relations

  • One steam account can have 0 to many Purchases(1 to m)
  • One purchase can have 0 to many products (1 to m)
